Race Typing

The goal of the game is to type as fast and as accurately as you can within the given time limit.

Players

You can pick either single player game or 2 player game.

Single Player

In single player game, you will be racing against a computer player. You can pick the difficulties of computer player (Easy, Medium, Hard, Extra Hard).
