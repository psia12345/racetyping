Race Typing

The goal of the game is to type as fast and as accurately as you can within the given time limit.

1 Player Instruction:

In single player game, you will be racing against a computer player. You can pick the difficulties of computer player (Easy, Medium, or Hard). Each difficulty is associated with different default wpm.

2 Players Instruction:

You can create a new 2 player game. Once you created the game, you have to wait until there is another player to join you. You can also join a random game, in which you will be matched with another player based on availability. You can also ask another friend to match with you using the unique game room ID that will be generated after created a new 2 player game. Each 2 player game will last 5 minutes. THe game will begin as soon as both players click 'Ready' button.
